Proposals for Preconference Workshops

The 2016 Code4lib Conference is now accepting proposals for pre-conference workshops. These pre-conferences can either be a 1-day or a 1/2-day workshop and will occur on Monday, March 7, 2016. Last year's sessions provide a good example of the content and style expected.

  • Workshops proposals will be acepted until midnight PST, November 15th
  • Confirmation of workshops will be communicated to leaders the first week of December.
  • If you run into any issues, email Christina at cmharlow AT gmail DOT com
