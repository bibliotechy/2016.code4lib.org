Diversity Scholarship Angel Fund

As part of the Code4Lib community's ongoing committment to helping under-represented groups excel in computing and technology, we are asking the community to help sponsor fellow community members, as selected by the Scholarship Committee, to attend the March 2016 Code4Lib Conference in Philadelphia, PA.

All funds raised will help pay for transportation, accommodation, and conference registration costs for Code4Lib's Diversity Scholarships recipients for our annual conference.
